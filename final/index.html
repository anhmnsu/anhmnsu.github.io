Action for Clean Water

Do You Know?

Surrounding us are four vast oceans. We have many beautiful and clear beaches spread across the country. Moreover, we have many rivers with majestic waterfalls and lakes full of seafood. In short, mother nature is very fond of human beings with precious, clear, and fresh resources. However, we are inadvertently destroying this precious gift, making these water sources terrifyingly polluted. Garbage strewn on the water. Stinky smells.

Together! We Make Changes

To recreate the natural environment, lakes, rivers and streams, bring fresh air and clean natural landscapes, help children and families have more outdoor play and entertainment areas, we join hands with the community to set up events for the environment and community.
